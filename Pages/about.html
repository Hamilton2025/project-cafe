About Us

Welcome to Local Business Café located in Salford Quays, Manchester — where great coffee meets community spirit.
We’re more than just a café — we’re a cozy space for connection, creativity, and comfort. Whether you're grabbing your morning espresso, meeting friends for brunch, or finding a quiet corner to work, our café is designed to make you feel at home. We proudly serve locally roasted coffee, fresh pastries, and wholesome meals made with love. Stop by, sip slowly, and stay awhile — we’re here to make every visit feel special.
